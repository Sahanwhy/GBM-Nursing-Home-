About Us

Where Healing Meets Excellence

G.B.M. Nursing Home has been a cornerstone of healthcare in the Golaghat district of Assam since 1994. Located near the Stadium in Golaghat town, we combine the warmth of a community hospital with the precision of advanced medical technology.

From ICU & NICU care to 24×7 diagnostics, our multidisciplinary team is committed to delivering world-class treatment close to home.
